# Xiang Ma Profile Content Notes

This file keeps the researcher profile material in reusable form for future website updates. It is intentionally written as factual content blocks rather than promotional copy.

## Research Directions

### Medical and Optical Metrology

- Work on defocus microstructure lenses connects image acquisition, defect detection, physical scale calibration, and software tools for lens inspection.
- Current National Key R&D work studies wear detection and key-parameter assessment for defocus microstructure lenses. The work uses dark-field microscopy images, defect annotations, object detection, segmentation, scoring, and a GUI-oriented inspection workflow.
- The current software/code basis is reflected in `DM_Scan` and `Microlens_DF`.
- Related OCT work includes contact lens geometric measurement and ophthalmic OCT lateral-resolution metrology. The software basis is reflected in `OCT_LatRes`.
- Related microscopy work includes STEM atomic-position analysis and publication-ready displacement/strain visualisation. The software basis is reflected in `STEM_atomap_GUI`.

### Human-Computer Interaction and Visual Recognition

- Earlier work uses conjunctival vascular images for gaze tracking, eye-movement measurement, and non-contact physiological signal extraction.
- The Zhejiang Natural Science Foundation project studied layered conjunctival vascular recognition and eye tracking from 2020-01 to 2023-12.
- The authorised invention patent `CN115984952B` is for an eye tracking system and method based on conjunctival vascular image recognition.
- Teaching and supervision extend this direction into HCI coursework, LLM-assisted grading, pose-based movement comparison, and applied visual recognition tools.
- The current software/code basis is reflected in `HCI-AutoGrader`, `DanceBattle`, `WaveGazeZoo`, and `WDMPA-Net`.
- `WDMPA-Net` implements a compact appearance-based gaze-estimation model with wavelet downsampling, multi-scale attention, ablation summaries, ONNX export, and Jetson benchmarking utilities.
- A Fashion Similarity Detection Tool for Clothing Company was developed for clothing-target detection and similarity retrieval from e-commerce screenshots, supporting apparel-brand monitoring and similarity review.

### Machine Learning for Energy, Environment, and Quality Systems

- Work includes photovoltaic and solar irradiance forecasting, building energy forecasting, chiller fault diagnosis, air-quality forecasting, and predictive-maintenance planning.
- Research methods include LSTM variants, Transformer-style models, reinforcement learning, diffusion/generative models, graph modelling, and Mamba-based sequence models.
- The current software/code basis is reflected in `IntelligentMaintenancePlanning`, `CMamba-AAE`, `DMGENet`, and related forecasting repositories. `DMGENet` uses distance, neighborhood, distribution-similarity, and functional-similarity station graphs with reinforcement-learning model combination for multi-station PM2.5 forecasting.

### Computer Science Education and Student Mentoring

- Teaching covers computer organization and design, C programming, Python programming, applied HCI, digital image processing, and computer frontier topics.
- Course materials combine fundamentals with experiment design, programming practice, image algorithms, hardware/software interfaces, and project-style assessment.
- Supervision includes 19 MSc students, with 13 graduated students and 6 students currently in training in 2026.
- Since 2024-09, Xiang Ma has served as the class adviser for the 2024 full-time master's cohort in Computer Science and Technology, covering 69 students.

## Grants and Funded Projects

- National Key R&D Program of China, `2024YFC2419504`, "Research on Wear Degree Detection and Key Parameter Assessment of Defocus Microstructure Lenses", 2024-12 to 2027-01. The current work is in progress. China Jiliang University participates in metrology modelling, dark-field image datasets, defect detection/segmentation, scoring, and inspection software. The related public software basis is `DM_Scan` and `Microlens_DF`.
- National Key R&D Program of China, `2021YFC3340402`, "Research on Evidence Collection Technology for Infringing and Counterfeit Goods in Typical Application Scenarios", 2022-11 to 2024-11, RMB 800,000. Xiang Ma participated as a fixed/key researcher in trademark detection technology, contributed to proposal drafting and application preparation, served as the overall technical secretary during project reviews and inspections, organised milestone and outcome materials, and developed the Fashion Similarity Detection Tool for Clothing Company.
- Zhejiang Natural Science Foundation / Basic Public Welfare Research, "Layered Segmentation Model of Conjunctival Vascular Network and Eye Tracking", 2020-01 to 2023-12. Xiang Ma served as PI. The project studied tissue-optics-informed conjunctival vascular layering, eye tracking, head-mounted measurement, and AI-based trajectory prediction. It produced the authorised patent `CN115984952B`.
